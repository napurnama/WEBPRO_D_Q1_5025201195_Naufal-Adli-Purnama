Alun-Alun Magelang

Alun-alun (aloon-aloon) became the city’s center because of its strategic location and ease of access. It is a major recreation destination where residents of Magelang often spend the afternoon. It is the gateway to shopping in Magelang, especially due to its proximity to Pecinan (Chinatown). Northwest of the alun-alun stands a 15m water tower which is a relic of Dutch Colonization. The water tower is still operational, used to store the residents’ drinking water.
